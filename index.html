Welcome

Much of the pine straw harvested in the United States comes from North Florida and South Georgia. Our business is located in the heart of South Georgia — Sylvester, Georgia — right in the middle of prime pine straw country.

We sell premium longleaf and slash pine straw in square bales and round rolls, delivered all over the Southeast United States. Whether you're a landscaper, nursery, golf course, commercial property, or homeowner, we've got you covered.

We look forward to doing business with you.

Our Services

Longleaf & Slash Pine Straw

We only bale longleaf and slash pine straw — the highest quality varieties available.

Square Bales & Round Rolls

Available in both square bales and round rolls to fit your project needs.

Delivery Across the Southeast

We deliver throughout Georgia, Alabama, Florida, the Carolinas, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Louisiana.

Hand-Baled Quality

All bales are hand-baled using metal box balers for consistent, tight bales every time.

Own & Leased Fields

We only sell straw harvested from fields we own or lease — guaranteeing superior quality from source to delivery.

Commercial & Residential

Serving landscapers, nurseries, golf courses, schools, churches, colleges, homeowners, and commercial properties.
